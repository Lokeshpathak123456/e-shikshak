Our Mission

At E Shikshak, our mission is to provide free access to coding resources for students around the world. We believe that anyone should be able to learn to code, regardless of their background or financial situation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every student has the skills to thrive in a digital world. Through our platform, we aim to create a community of learners who are passionate about coding and technology.
